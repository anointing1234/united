About Us

We've Been Thriving in 15 Years The Area

BankUnited is primarily a retail banking operation, providing credit cards, auto loans and home loans in addition to checking and savings services. Originally a pure credit card company, BankUnited expanded into banking in earnest in 2005. Early in 2014 BankUnited was criticized for changing its customer terms of service to allow the bank to contact customers in any manner it chose, including deliberately deceiving them with fake phone caller IDs. Today, BankUnited has more than $318 billion in assets, though its branches number only 474, the lowest count on this list.

BankUnited, we strive to build on our community bank heritage by combining high ethical standards with innovative technologies to deliver truly exceptional service to our clients and communities. We are committed to implementing new ideas and cultivating relationships that encourage our communities, large and small, to grow and thrive.

Our History

BankUnited holds the oldest continuously operating bank charter in the United States. Acquired through Wachovia, it was originally granted to the BankUnited of Oklahoma, USA. In 2016, the bank gained some notoriety after news came out about a corporate culture where employees felt pressured to create 2 million bank accounts without customers’ permission.

Our Mission

We will create and maintain a professional environment that invites the ideas of our employees, fosters the confidence of our shareholders, and exceeds the expectations of our customers. We creates a positive economic impact in our communities.

Our Vision

To be a financially viable, independent community bank that is committed to improving the quality of life of the communities we serve. To earn the loyalty of employees, customers and the community by operating with integrity and fairness at all times.
